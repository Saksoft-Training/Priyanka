Overview

This Movies App is designed to help movie lovers explore, add, and keep track of their favorite films. Whether you're looking for recommendations, detailed movie info, or want to curate your own list, this app makes it simple and enjoyable.

Features

  • Browse recommended movies with poster images and year of release.
  • Add new movies to your personal collection easily.
  • View detailed information about any movie in your list.
  • Delete movies you no longer want in your collection.
  • Responsive design for a seamless experience on all devices.
